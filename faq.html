FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Yes, it can but it is inefficient to use mantle lamps. For that we recommend you use the gas to run our Bio DC generator. This will simultaneously charge automotive batteries which in turn will power lights, radio, TV, charge phones etc.

Dung from 1 cow is enough for the average 6-8-member homestead. All you need is 20 – 30 Kgs of cow dung per day.

Dung from pigs, sheep, goats, rabbits, chicken droppings or any organic waste, including kitchen waste, water hyacinth, weeds and market waste etc.

For continuous production of gas, you need to feed the system regularly depending on gas usage and what you feed in. It is therefore best for you to keep to a regular routine of once a day or twice a week.

Un-used gas will accumulate in the digester. However, if un-used for a long time, the slurry will be ejected from the outlet.

The system utilizes cross-flow technology making it maintenance free. Fully digested bio-slurry will be automatically ejected from the outlet.

The waste (bio-slurry) ejected has no smell. It is in fact a very nutritious organic fertilizer for crops that has been completely broken down ready for absorption by plants.

Exposed to the elements, the fabric is manufactured to last over ten years. The micro green-house tunnel further shields the digester from direct sunlight and helps maintain high temperatures to increase efficiency of biogas production. The lifespan can be further enhanced by applying UV sun-screen every 5-7 years.
