BIO-SAN-GAS
BIO-SANITATION Solution

Our Bio-San systems are expertly crafted to provide hygienic sanitation solutions, effectively managing human waste in a safe and environmentally responsible manner.

Human waste management has been a challenge especially for institutions due to the number of students and the high cost of building the proper infrastructure to facilitate the same.

Building of latrines has worsened the situation because they get filled up really quick, produce bad odour and contaminate ground water due to percolation of the fecal fluid to the underground water. This exposes the students to diseases like typhoid and cholera putting their health at great risk.

BioSan System Overview

BioSan System - Key Advantages

Key Advantages of the Bio-San System:

  • Never fills up and never requires emptying, eliminating the need for exhausters.
  • Suitable for all terrain, so rocky areas and high water table areas is not a problem.
  • Cost-effective compared to conventional waste management methods.
  • Prefabricated for quick and easy installation.
  • The liquid byproduct can be used for tree irrigation or directed along fences.
  • Ensures a clean and sanitary environment.

Other Benefits

  • For institutions and large capacity use, the BioSanGas plants manage this waste and produces gas that can be used for heating purposes, running machinery such as chuff-cutters, hummer mills and for power generation.
  • The system comes in different sizes depending on the number of people that will be feeding the plant.
  • The plant also ensures complete digestion of the waste and the exiting liquid is full of nutrients and safe to use as fertilizer for the lawns and play ground, tree planting programs like fruit trees, live fencing and hedgerows, etc.
  • The BioSanGas system is best applicable for Institutions such as schools, churches, market places, children homes and prisons.

Anaerobic Grey Water Treatment for Showers and Kitchen Sinks

Our Bio-San system extends its capabilities to treat kitchen sink and shower water through an anaerobic grey water treatment plant. This processed water, treated with Effective Micro-organisms (EM), can be utilized for irrigation purposes.

Similar in design to our Bio-San system, this process guarantees complete fermentation, breaking down soaps and oils. The extended retention time of over 40 days ensures the complete elimination of pathogens, as confirmed by laboratory tests.

The Bio-San system and Anaerobic Grey Water Treatment Plant provide comprehensive solutions for sustainable waste management and water reuse, promoting a healthier and cleaner environment for all.

The Merry Loo - Hygienic Pit Latrine Replacement

I'd like to introduce you to our latest innovation.

Our aim is to promote hygienic human waste management systems that are simple and low cost replacement of the pit latrine toilets commonly found in rural and urban homesteads.

We aim to stop the contamination of our rivers and under ground water and to stop feeding nutrients to invasive plants like water hyacinth in Lake Victoria.

We've developed a simple low cost replacement for the pit latrine.

Because a lot of dignity and self esteem is associated with having a clean loo, we've nicknamed it The Merry Loo 😋

The Merry loo is a simple low cost replacement for the pit latrine. It mainly features a multi chamber digester to ensure total liquidation of solids and complete fermentation, which also means the system never fills up and never requires emptying.

The resultant liquid, now bio-fertiliser is fed through shallow French drains and plants are grown on top to take advantage of the 24/7 irrigation, as well as suck up the nutrients so they don't end up polluting under ground water bodies and in the rivers and lakes.

This also means the system never fills up and never requires emptying.

The systems are prefabricated and installation is quick and easy.

The system is installed above ground, so rocky areas and high water tables are not an issue.

This design will suit households in urban, peri-urban and in rural areas.

We are setting up training for youth groups and CBO's interested in taking this simple clean technology to the people.

The main Features include:
✅– Non-biodegradeable separator
✅– 7 stage fermenter
✅– Squat Asian style loo
✅– Shower basin
✅– EM priming culture

Ideal for rural homes of 5 – 6 members
