About our Impact-Driven & Inspirational

Welcome to Flexi Biogas — where waste becomes a solution. We design and deliver smart biogas systems that turn everyday organic waste into clean cooking gas and rich organic fertilizer. Whether you're a home, school, or farm, our systems are made to work for you — sustainably, affordably, and reliably.

Join us in building a cleaner future, one flame at a time. More about our journey

Copycat farming model

The by-product of our biogas systems, the bio-slurry, is a mineral-rich organic fertilizer ready for use. It serves as an effective pest repellent and can be applied directly to plants, promoting healthier crops and reducing reliance on chemical fertilizers.

To maximize the benefits of biogas, we've developed additional products like biogas dryers and brooders. These innovations allow users to utilize biogas for various applications, from drying fruits and vegetables to providing heat for poultry brooding, enhancing the versatility of our systems.​

By offering user-friendly biogas systems, we enable people to take control of their energy sources, reduce dependency on unsustainable fuels, and build healthier, more resilient lives. From kitchen leftovers to animal waste, we turn what many see as trash into a valuable, everyday solution.
