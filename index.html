About our Impact-Driven & Inspirational

Welcome to Flexi Biogas — where waste becomes a solution. We design and deliver smart biogas systems that turn everyday organic waste into clean cooking gas and rich organic fertilizer. Whether you're a home, school, or farm, our systems are made to work for you — sustainably, affordably, and reliably.

Join us in building a cleaner future, one flame at a time. More about our journey

T-REX TECHNOLOGY SYSTEMS

Large scale systems built according to energy demand need. Perfect for schools, farms, hotels, churches, markets, and children's homes.

FLEXI-BIOGAS-TECHNOLOGY

Systems designed for household energy demands. Installed in 3 hours, producing gas in 3-5 days, running on any biodegradable material.

BIO-SAN-GAS SYSTEMS

Clean hygienic solution producing both biogas and rich fertilizer. Prefabricated and assembled on site in hours.
