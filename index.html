About our Impact-Driven & Inspirational

Welcome to Flexi Biogas — where waste becomes a solution. We design and deliver smart biogas systems that turn everyday organic waste into clean cooking gas and rich organic fertilizer. Whether you're a home, school, or farm, our systems are made to work for you — sustainably, affordably, and reliably.

Join us in building a cleaner future, one flame at a time. More about our journey

T-REX TECHNOLOGY SYSTEMS

T-Rex Technology Systems are advanced, modular biogas plants designed for high-capacity energy needs in farms, schools, hotels, and institutions. These systems use a unique cross-flow fermentation process, ensuring complete digestion of all biodegradable materials. Prefabricated for quick installation and scalable to any size, T-Rex delivers maximum gas output and a constant supply of rich organic fertilizer.

FLEXI-BIOGAS-TECHNOLOGY

Flexi Biogas Technology offers a no-construction, plug-and-play biogas solution for homes and small businesses. Prefabricated and installed in as little as three hours, these systems run on any biodegradable material and produce clean cooking gas and organic fertilizer. The technology is user-friendly, quick to start, and adaptable to a wide range of environments.

Read More

BIO-SAN-GAS SYSTEMS

Bio-San-Gas Systems provide a hygienic, sustainable solution for human waste management in institutions and communities. These expertly crafted, prefabricated systems never fill up or require emptying, eliminating odors and groundwater contamination. They convert waste into biogas for heating and power, while producing safe, nutrient-rich liquid fertilizer for irrigation.

Copycat farming model

The CopyCat Farming Model is a climate-smart, replicable farming system that leverages biogas and bio-fertiliser to promote sustainable agriculture. By integrating innovative irrigation and fertilisation techniques, this model empowers farmers to increase productivity, reduce costs, and build resilience to climate change. Demonstration sites showcase practical solutions for communities to adopt.

Chicken Brooding using biogas

Our biogas-powered Chicken Brooding systems offer a sustainable, cost-effective alternative to traditional brooding methods. Using radiant floor heating or pollutant-free space heaters, these systems maintain optimal temperatures for chicks while reducing energy costs. Suitable for both commercial and domestic setups, they improve poultry health and support circular farming.

Drying Fruits & Vegetables

Our biogas-powered Drying Fruits & Vegetables systems help farmers preserve their harvest, reduce post-harvest losses, and add value to their produce. The dryers use biogas burners to gently dehydrate fruits and vegetables, extending shelf life and enabling sales during off-seasons. Dried products retain nutrients and provide new market opportunities.

POWERING COMMUNITIES. PROTECTING THE PLANET.

We exist to bridge the gap between waste and energy. At Flexi Biogas, our mission is to make renewable energy a reality for households, institutions, and farms — no matter their size or location. By offering user-friendly biogas systems, we enable people to take control of their energy sources, reduce dependency on unsustainable fuels, and build healthier, more resilient lives. From kitchen leftovers to animal waste, we turn what many see as trash into a valuable, everyday solution.

CLEAN ENERGY IN EVERY HOME, ONE SYSTEM AT A TIME.

We envision a future where biogas is not the alternative — but the norm. Our goal is to expand the reach of clean energy to every corner of the continent, and beyond. In this future, smoke-filled kitchens are a thing of the past, organic waste is no longer wasted, and families thrive in energy-secure environments. With every system installed, we take a step toward a cleaner, fairer, and more sustainable world.
